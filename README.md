# Nigerian Car Market Analysis

## Project Overview
An analysis of ~500 vehicle listings across Nigeria to identify pricing trends, popular brands, and regional market hubs.

## Key Insights (from Excel Analysis)
* **Market Leader:** **Toyota** dominates the market with 187 units, followed by **Mercedes-Benz** (60) and **Lexus** (54).
* **Regional Hubs:** **Lagos** is the primary market center (295 cars), followed by **Federal Capital Territory** (70) and **Oyo** (50).
